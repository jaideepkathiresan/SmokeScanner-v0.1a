Smoke Scanner v0.1a
------------------------------------------------------------------- Smoke Scanner v0.1a by team CCP ------------------------------------------------------------------- Smoke Scanner is a good-willed academic project that seeks to control climate change by helping authorities identify and control emission norms violators, which is a huge & unchecked menace in many developing countries. Go ahead and add in a video/image or a live stream file (in .m3u8 format) to start scanning for visible or excess smoke emissions. if unnatural emissions are detected, its logged in this terminal and also a frame of the detected vehicle is stored on your disk. -------------------------------------------------------------------
